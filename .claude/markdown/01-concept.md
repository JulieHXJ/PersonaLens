# Nightshift — Product Concept

## One-Liner
Autonomous AI customer discovery engine that runs 50+ simulated customer interviews overnight and delivers a structured market intelligence report by morning.

## The Problem
The #1 reason startups fail (42%, CB Insights) is "no market need." Founders build what they think people want instead of validating first.

Real customer discovery is:
- **Slow:** Finding 50 people in your target segment takes weeks
- **Expensive:** Scheduling and conducting 50 interviews takes months
- **Awkward:** Most founders skip it entirely and just build
- **Biased:** Even when they do interviews, they ask leading questions and hear what they want to hear

Result: founders burn 6-12 months building something nobody wants.

## The Solution
Replace the most painful part of the startup journey with an autonomous, overnight batch process.

You don't chat with Nightshift. You don't sit and watch it. You configure a run, press go, and go to sleep. By morning, you have a comprehensive market intelligence report based on 50+ simulated customer discovery interviews.

## Key Differentiators
- **Not a chatbot** — it's a batch processing system with autonomous agents
- **Synthetic persona generation** with diversity constraints (income distribution, skepticism calibration, segment quotas)
- **Multi-turn interview simulation** with adaptive branching logic
- **Cross-persona pattern detection** and statistical analysis
- **Structured data extraction** from unstructured interviews

## Target Users
- Startup founders validating ideas
- Product managers exploring new markets
- Innovation teams at enterprises
- Solo entrepreneurs
- Hackathon participants (meta: we eat our own cooking)

## Business Model
- Freemium: 1 free run (10 personas), paid plans for more
- Per-run pricing or monthly subscription
- Unit economics: ~$0.50-2.00 in API costs per 50-persona run, charge $19-49

## Market Size
- Customer research tools: $2B+
- Startup validation: $500M+
- Every founder, PM, and innovation team is a potential user
